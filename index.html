Ghana

Ghana, officially the Republic of Ghana, is a country in West Africa known for its democratic governance, rich cultural heritage, and diverse geography including tropical rainforests and coastal savannas. The country is rich in natural resources and is a leading producer of cocoa and gold. With its capital in Accra, Ghana is a significant regional player, noted for its history as the first Black African nation south of the Sahara to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957.
